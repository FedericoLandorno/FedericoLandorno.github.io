Hi! I'm Federico, a UX Designer and developer. At the moment I am pursuing a Master in Interactive Media Technology from the Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm to deepen my knowledge of user experience and interaction.

I started my journey as a developer during high school where I learned the fundamentals of logic. Spurred by the challenges, I started working in this area as a Java developer until I decided to resume studying by enrolling at the University of Turin. In those three years I learned new approaches to programming and got a peripheral view on the vast subject.

I decided to move on to a branch that requires more imagination and relationships with people, so I decided to have an experience abroad in Sweden where I now live and study!
